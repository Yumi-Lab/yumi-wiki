# Components & Accessories

Add-on boards that extend the Smart Pi One — a touchscreen for display and input, a USB hub for power and connectivity, and a shield for extra power and GPIO access. Each one clips onto the Smart Pi One with no soldering.

## At a glance

| Component | What it does | Key interfaces | Size | Weight |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [**Smart LCD Touch 4.3"**](SmartLCD_Touch_4.3inch800x480specifications.md) | 800×480 capacitive touchscreen (also a standalone HDMI monitor) | HDMI · FFC 20-pin (video) · FFC 6-pin (touch) | 106 × 85 mm | 88 g |
| [**Smart Hub 1→4**](SmartHub_specifications.md) | 1 USB input → 4 outputs, 5 V injected on every port | USB 2.0 ×3 · FFC 6-pin ×2 | 49.5 × 47.5 mm | 12 g |
| [**Smart Shield**](SmartShield_specifications.md) | USB-C power (doubles it) + 45° GPIO + UART / ADXL345 | USB-C · GPIO 40-pin · RJ11 · FFC 6-pin | 11.5 × 84 mm | 23 g |

The Smart Hub and Smart Shield are designed to work together — the Shield brings USB-C, the Hub fans it out to your peripherals.
