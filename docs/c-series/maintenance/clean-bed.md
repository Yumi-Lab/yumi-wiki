# Clean Print Bed

**Interval:** Every 7 days  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Time:** 2 minutes  
**Applies to:** C235, C335, C435

## Why

Fingerprints, dust, and filament residue reduce bed adhesion. A clean bed means:

- Better first layer adhesion
- No warping or lifting
- Consistent results across prints

## What You Need

- Isopropyl alcohol (IPA) 90%+
- Lint-free cloth or paper towel

## Steps

### 1. Let the bed cool
Wait until the bed is at room temperature.

### 2. Spray IPA
Spray or apply IPA generously on the build surface.

### 3. Wipe
Wipe in one direction with the lint-free cloth. Don't scrub in circles.

### 4. Let dry
Wait 30 seconds for the IPA to evaporate completely.

### 5. Mark as done
On your SmartPad: **Menu > More > Maintenance > Clean Print Bed > Done**

## Tips

- Don't touch the bed with bare fingers after cleaning
- For stubborn residue (PETG, glue stick), use warm water first then IPA
- Deep clean with dish soap and water every month
